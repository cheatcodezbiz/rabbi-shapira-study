月亮與太陽之戰 ── 普珥節、《以賽亞書》二十四章與以東的崩塌 War of Moon and Sun — Purim, Isaiah 24, and the Collapse of Edom

在一場重病中神向沙皮拉拉比啟示：《以賽亞書》二十四章中的「Hitpo'rer」(崩解) 字根 Pur，正是「普珥節」的起源——而 10 月 7 日所開啟的「陣痛」將在普珥節達到高峰，引發「月亮與太陽之戰」。 While gravely ill, God revealed to Rabbi Shapira the missing prophetic key in Isaiah 24: the Hebrew word Hitpo'rer (“disintegration”) carries the root Pur — the origin of Purim. The “contraction” that began on October 7th will reach its climax on Purim, igniting a “war of moon and sun.”

沙皮拉拉比博士 · Ahavat Ammi 事工 Rabbi Dr. Itzhak Shapira · Ahavat Ammi Ministries

「我地要東倒西歪，好像醉酒的人。」(以賽亞書 24:20) 這是先知以賽亞描寫末世「列國醒酒之日」的著名經文。拉比沙皮拉指出：這節中「崩解」這個字 Hitpo'rer（התפורר），其字根正是 Pur（פור）——也就是「普珥節」(Purim) 的字源。他在重病期間蒙啟示——這個古老字根中藏著一段先知性的密碼：那從 2023 年 10 月 7 日所開啟的「陣痛」，將在普珥節這一天達到高峰。更深的層次——以賽亞接著說：「月亮將要蒙羞，太陽將要慚愧」（24:23）——按拉什 (Rashi) 與阿巴貝爾 (Abarbanel) 的解讀，月亮代表伊斯蘭，太陽代表基督教世界。這場戰爭不只是以色列與伊朗，而是整個列國體系的洗牌。 “The earth will reel to and fro like a drunkard.” (Isaiah 24:20) — Isaiah's famous picture of the day the nations sober up at the end of the age. Rabbi Shapira's observation: the word for “disintegrate” here, Hitpo'rer (התפורר), is built on the root Pur (פור) — the same root from which Purim takes its name. While gravely ill, the rabbi received what he calls a divine reopening of this passage: a prophetic code carried inside the Hebrew letters. The “contraction” that opened on 7 October 2023 will reach its climax on Purim. And one verse later, Isaiah continues: “The moon shall be disgraced and the sun put to shame” (24:23) — which Rashi and Abarbanel both read as Islam (the moon) and the Christian nations (the sun). The war is not only Israel-vs-Iran. It is a reordering of the whole system of nations.

一、病榻上的啟示I. Revelation from a Sickbed

拉比沙皮拉以一段個人見證開場。在一場重病期間——他自己也不確定能否痊癒——神向他說話，指引他回到《以賽亞書》第二十四章，並重新打開他自己 2024 年所寫的那本書《新哈瑪斯》(New Hamas)。 Rabbi Shapira opens with a personal testimony. While gravely ill — uncertain whether he would recover — he says God spoke to him, directing him back to Isaiah 24 and back to his own 2024 book, New Hamas.

他說：神告訴他「有一段你之前沒看見的連結，現在我要打開給你」——一段把 10 月 7 日的事件、目前的伊朗戰局、與彌賽亞的降臨綁在一起的連結。它藏在以賽亞書 24 章的希伯來字根中。 He says: God told him “there is a connection you missed before; now I am opening it to you” — a connection that ties October 7, the present war with Iran, and the coming of the Messiah into one prophetic chain. It is hidden in the Hebrew roots of Isaiah 24.

二、《以賽亞書》24:20 的「崩解」── 字根中的普珥II. Isaiah 24:20 — the “Disintegration” That Carries Purim

「地要東倒西歪，好像醉酒的人；又搖來搖去，好像吊床。罪過在其上沉重，必然塌陷 (Hitpo'rer)，不能復起。」——以賽亞書 24:20 “The earth shall reel to and fro like a drunkard; it shall sway like a hammock. Its transgression shall lie heavy upon it, and it shall fall (Hitpo'rer) and not rise again.”— Isaiah 24:20

「塌陷」這個字 Hitpo'rer（התפורר）的字根是 פ-ו-ר（Pur）——意為「破碎、瓦解、潰散」。同樣這個字根，是「普珥節」(Purim) 名字的來源。普珥節紀念以斯帖時代神在波斯把哈曼的計謀「翻轉」過來——所謂「Pur」就是哈曼擲下要滅以色列的「籤」（以斯帖記 3:7）。 The word for “fall apart,” Hitpo'rer (התפורר), is built on the root פ-ו-ר (Pur) — “to break, dissolve, scatter.” That same root names the festival of Purim. Purim commemorates Persia of Esther's day, where God overturned Haman's plot — the “Pur” that names the festival is the very “lot” Haman cast to destroy Israel (Esther 3:7).

拉比沙皮拉的應用：以賽亞書描寫末世「世界塌陷」的這個動詞，在希伯來文裡內含「普珥」這個節日的字根。換句話說：先知用同一個字根告訴我們那個塌陷將在哪一天達到高峰——在普珥，而且從哪一片土地開始——古代普珥節故事所發生的土地，即波斯／伊朗。 Rabbi Shapira's application: the verb Isaiah uses for the end-time disintegration of the world contains, in its Hebrew root, the name of the festival of Purim. The prophet, in other words, encoded both when the climax of the disintegration falls (Purim) and where the chain begins (the soil of the original Purim story — Persia / Iran).

三、24:21-22 ── 「在地的列王、在高處的眾軍，被聚集在 Bor（坑）裡」III. Isaiah 24:21–22 — Kings of the Earth Gathered in the Bor

「到那日，耶和華在高處必懲罰高處的眾軍，在地上必懲罰地上的列王。他們必被聚集，像囚犯在監牢裡 (Bor)，並要囚在牢獄中，多日之後便被討罪。」——以賽亞書 24:21-22 “In that day the LORD will punish the host of heaven in heaven, and the kings of the earth on the earth. They shall be gathered together, as prisoners are gathered in the pit (Bor); they shall be shut up in prison; and after many days they shall be visited.”— Isaiah 24:21-22

希伯來文「Bor」(בור) 意思是「坑、監牢、深井」。這個字在約瑟記 (創世記 37) 中被用——約瑟被丟進坑裡。在拉比傳統中，Bor 帶有強烈的「被囚禁、被埋葬、暫時消失」的意味。 The Hebrew Bor (בור) means “pit, dungeon, deep well.” The same word is used for the pit Joseph was thrown into (Genesis 37). In rabbinic tradition Bor carries strong connotations of imprisonment, burial, temporary disappearance.

拉比沙皮拉指出：以賽亞書 24:22 中「列王與眾軍被聚集在 Bor」這個畫面，正是 2023 年 10 月 7 日所開啟的——以色列被囚的人民、世界的領袖們困在他們自己的政治泥潭中、伊朗政權陷入它自己挖的坑。「他們將在多日之後被討罪。」那個「多日」的盡頭——按米大示——是普珥。 Rabbi Shapira's observation: this picture in Isaiah 24:22 — kings and hosts being gathered in a Bor — is what opened on 7 October 2023. Israel's hostages held in literal pits; the world's leaders trapped in their own political pits; Iran's regime sinking into the pit it dug for others. “And after many days they shall be visited.” The end of those “many days” — by the midrash's clock — is Purim.

四、24:23 ── 月亮與太陽 (Levanah ve'Chamah)IV. Isaiah 24:23 — Moon and Sun (Levanah ve'Chamah)

「那時月亮要蒙羞 (V'hafra ha'Levanah)，太陽要慚愧 (U'voshah ha'Chamah)，因為萬軍之耶和華必在錫安山、在耶路撒冷作王，在祂面前的長老必有榮耀。」——以賽亞書 24:23 “Then the moon will be disgraced (V'hafra ha'Levanah), and the sun put to shame (U'voshah ha'Chamah); for the LORD of hosts will reign on Mount Zion and in Jerusalem, and before His elders will be glory.”— Isaiah 24:23

拉什（Rashi，11世紀）與阿巴貝爾（Abarbanel，15世紀）的注釋一致：「月亮」(Levanah) 代表以實瑪利的後裔——伊斯蘭世界（伊斯蘭信仰以月亮為其象徵；伊斯蘭曆是純陰曆）。「太陽」(Chamah) 代表以掃／以東的後裔——基督教世界（基督教世界使用陽曆；羅馬帝國以太陽神為其崇拜對象）。 Rashi (11th c.) and Abarbanel (15th c.) agree: “moon” (Levanah) represents the descendants of Ishmael — the Islamic world (Islam takes the moon as its symbol; the Islamic calendar is purely lunar). “Sun” (Chamah) represents the descendants of Esau / Edom — the Christian nations (the Christian world uses the solar calendar; Rome built itself on solar deities).

因此，以賽亞書 24:23 所描述的不只是天文奇觀——它是「月亮與太陽之戰」，即伊斯蘭世界與基督教世界的對撞。當神要在錫安作王時，這兩個世界都將「蒙羞」、「慚愧」——意味著它們各自的政治宗教體系都將在彌賽亞王前露出局限。 So Isaiah 24:23 describes not an astronomical curiosity but “a war of moon and sun” — the collision of the Islamic world with the Christian world. When God reigns on Zion, both will be “disgraced” and “put to shame” — meaning both political-religious systems will be exposed in their limits before the Messiah-King.

五、V'hafra 與 Hafar ── 「挖坑」的字根V. V'hafra and Hafar — The Root of “Digging”

拉比沙皮拉指出 24:23 中「月亮蒙羞」這個動詞——V'hafra（וְחָפְרָה）——它的字根是 ח-פ-ר（Hafar），意思是「挖、挖坑」。這個字根與 24:22 中那個 Bor（坑）形成希伯來文中的閉環：那「挖坑」者，自己被困在坑中。 Rabbi Shapira notes that the verb in 24:23 for “the moon will be disgraced” — V'hafra (וְחָפְרָה) — is built on the root ח-פ-ר (Hafar), “to dig, to dig a pit.” That root closes a Hebrew loop with the Bor of 24:22: the one who dug the pit ends up trapped in it.

他應用這個字根：今天伊朗（和以實瑪利的代理人們）在加薩、黎巴嫩、敘利亞挖了密集的地下隧道網絡——這正是字面意義上的 Hafar。詩篇 7:15 已說：「他掘了坑，又挖深了，竟掉在自己所挖的阱裡。」這個古老格言以希伯來字根的形式，再一次寫入今日的新聞。 His application: today's Iran (and Ishmael's proxies) have built dense networks of underground tunnels in Gaza, Lebanon, and Syria — literal Hafar. Psalm 7:15 already says: “He digs a pit, hollows it deep, and falls into the pit he made.” That ancient maxim, in its Hebrew root form, has been written into today's headlines.

六、Klipa 與 Sitra Achra ── 殼的破碎VI. Klipa and Sitra Achra — The Shattering of the Shell

在卡巴拉（猶太神秘主義）傳統中，Klipa（קליפה，「殼」）與Sitra Achra（סטרא אחרא，「另一面／邪惡勢力」）是用來描述「包圍神聖光的邪惡外殼」的詞彙。在末世，這些殼將被神親自打破。 In Kabbalistic tradition, Klipa (קליפה, “shell”) and Sitra Achra (סטרא אחרא, “the other side / evil forces”) describe the evil husks that surround divine light. In the end-times, those husks will be broken open by God Himself.

拉比沙皮拉的應用：今天我們所看見的「邪惡聯盟」——伊朗、真主黨、哈瑪斯、以及那些公開站在以色列對立面的勢力——按猶太傳統，是 Klipa 的當代形式。它們的崩塌（Hitpo'rer）不是政治偶然，而是末世圖景的一部分；它們的崩塌，會立即引出一個「聖潔的聯盟」——以色列、餘民、以及義人列國的同盟。 Rabbi Shapira's application: today's “unholy coalition” — Iran, Hezbollah, Hamas, and those who publicly stand against Israel — is, by the Jewish tradition, the contemporary form of Klipa. Their disintegration (Hitpo'rer) is not political accident; it is part of the end-time picture. Their fall will immediately reveal a “holy alliance” — Israel, the remnant of the Jewish people, and the righteous of the nations standing together.

七、對基督徒的意義VII. What This Means for Christians

第一，「太陽要慚愧」這節經文對基督徒是一個極嚴肅的提醒。拉什明確把「太陽」讀作基督教世界。當神在錫安作王時，連基督教世界——包括它在歷史中所建立的權力結構、神學自滿、與對以色列的長期錯待——都將「慚愧」。這不是說真信仰會被廢去，而是那些被人加上的政治宗教外殼會被打破。 First, the verse “the sun will be put to shame” is a sober word to Christians. Rashi reads “sun” as the Christian nations. When God reigns on Zion, even the Christian world — including its historic power structures, theological self-satisfaction, and the long mistreatment of Israel — will be “put to shame.” This does not say true faith will be abolished. It says the political-religious shells men have added to it will be broken open.

第二，那個「聖潔的聯盟」——以色列加上列國的義人——是基督徒在這個末世時刻被邀請進入的位置。拉比沙皮拉的呼召：「歸向以色列的神，雅各的神，加入這聖潔的聯盟」。對華人基督徒來說，這意味著與以色列同站、與耶書亞的猶太弟兄們同站，預備進入尼散月的救贖。 Second, that “holy alliance” — Israel together with the righteous of the nations — is the place Christians are invited into in this end-time hour. Rabbi Shapira's call: “turn to the God of Israel, the God of Jacob, and join this holy alliance.” For Chinese Christians, this means standing with Israel, standing with Yeshua's Jewish brothers, preparing to enter the redemption of Nisan.

第三，「他掘了坑，又挖深了，竟掉在自己所挖的阱裡」這節詩篇，提醒我們：神並不需要我們替祂扳倒邪惡。我們的功課是不要陷入挖坑的試探——不要用恨的方式對抗恨，不要用詭詐的方式對抗詭詐。讓神自己打破那些殼。 Third, Psalm 7:15 — “he falls into the pit he made” — reminds us that God does not need us to topple the evil ourselves. Our work is not to be drawn into the temptation of digging — not to fight hate with hate, not to fight deceit with deceit. Leave God to break the shells.

八、關於分辨VIII. A Word on Discernment

拉比沙皮拉所運用的字根聯想（Hitpo'rer ↔ Pur，V'hafra ↔ Hafar）是Drash——一種猶太傳統中極為豐富的解經方式，建立在希伯來文字根的多義性之上。它的價值在於揭示經文中的神學共鳴，但不應被讀為「字面預言哪一年哪一日會發生什麼」。 The root-association readings Rabbi Shapira works with (Hitpo'rer ↔ Pur; V'hafra ↔ Hafar) are drash — a rich Jewish interpretive form built on the polysemy of Hebrew roots. Its value is to reveal theological resonance, not to give us a calendar that predicts what happens on what day.

話雖如此，拉什與阿巴貝爾對「月亮 = 伊斯蘭、太陽 = 基督教世界」的解讀，不是沙皮拉個人的創造——這是猶太古典解經傳統中的標準讀法。當我們今天看見伊斯蘭世界與基督教世界都在這個末世時刻被深刻挑戰時，這節經文的應用是合理的，而不是穿鑿。 That said, the “moon = Islam, sun = the Christian nations” reading by Rashi and Abarbanel is not Rabbi Shapira's invention — it is a standard line in classical Jewish exegesis. As we watch both the Islamic world and the Christian world being profoundly challenged in this end-time hour, the application of this verse is reasonable, not strained.

九、結語 ── 加入聖潔的聯盟IX. Closing — Join the Holy Alliance

「歸向以色列的神，雅各的神。加入這聖潔的聯盟。預備你的心進入尼散月。彌賽亞的降臨已近。Am Yisrael Chai。」 “Turn to the God of Israel, the God of Jacob. Join this holy alliance. Prepare your heart for Nisan. The coming of Messiah is near. Am Yisrael Chai.”
