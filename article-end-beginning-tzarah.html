末日的開端 ── Tzarah、雅各的患難與全球性的覺醒 The Beginning of the End — Tzarah, Jacob's Trouble, and a Global Awakening

沙皮拉拉比指出：當以色列、伊朗、沙特、美國（以東）的利益在全球舞台上彼此撞擊時，我們正在進入「雅各的患難」(Tzarat Yaakov) 的最後階段。希伯來文 Tzarah（צרה，「患難」）的字根 Tzar（צר，「窄」、「圍困」），預言著苦難之後的大轉向。 As the interests of Israel, Iran, Saudi Arabia, and America (Edom) collide on the world stage, Rabbi Shapira locates us in the final phase of “Jacob's Trouble” (Tzarat Yaakov). The Hebrew Tzarah (צרה, “distress”) is built on the root Tzar (צר, “narrow,” “besieging”) — and the prophets attach to that distress a coming great turning of the nations.

沙皮拉拉比博士 · Ahavat Ammi 事工 Rabbi Dr. Itzhak Shapira · Ahavat Ammi Ministries

「當你在末後的日子遭遇一切患難 (Tzarah) 之時，你必歸向耶和華你的神，聽從祂的話。」(申命記 4:30)拉比沙皮拉以這節經文打開這篇教導——它是猶太傳統理解末世的鑰匙：「Tzarah」(צרה，「患難」) 不是結局，而是歸向的契機。希伯來文 Tzarah 的字根是 Tzar（צר，「窄」、「圍困」）——同一個我們在霍爾木茲、Botsra、Sitra Achra 中所看見的字根。拉比的判斷：今日以色列、伊朗、沙特、美國（以東）的利益在全球舞台上彼此撞擊時——我們正在「雅各的患難」(Tzarat Yaakov) 之中。並且根據申命記 4:30 的應許：在這 Tzarah 之後，必有大規模的歸向。 “When you are in distress (Tzarah) and all these things have come upon you in the latter days, you will return to the LORD your God and obey His voice.” (Deuteronomy 4:30) Rabbi Shapira opens with this verse — the Jewish tradition's key for reading the end. “Tzarah” (צרה, “distress”) is not the ending; it is the moment of turning. The Hebrew Tzarah is built on the root Tzar (צר, “narrow,” “besieging”) — the same root we have already met in Hormuz, Botsra, and the Sitra Achra. The rabbi's judgement: as Israel, Iran, Saudi Arabia, and America (Edom) collide on the world stage today — we are now inside Jacob's Trouble (Tzarat Yaakov). And by Deuteronomy 4:30's promise, what follows the Tzarah is a mass turning of the nations.

一、為什麼把今日地緣政治讀為「雅各的患難」？I. Why Read Today's Geopolitics as “Jacob's Trouble”?

「雅各的患難」(Tzarat Yaakov，צרת יעקב) 出自耶利米書 30:7：「哀哉！那日為大，無日可比，這是雅各遭難的時候，但他必被救出來。」這節經文在猶太傳統與基督教傳統中都被讀為末世的關鍵時刻——以色列被四面圍困、卻在最危急時被神親自救出。 “Jacob's Trouble” (Tzarat Yaakov, צרת יעקב) comes from Jeremiah 30:7: “Alas! that day is great, none is like it; it is the time of Jacob's trouble — yet out of it he shall be saved.” Both Jewish and Christian traditions read this as the pivotal moment of the end — Israel hemmed in on every side, then rescued by God Himself at the last possible moment.

拉比沙皮拉指出，今日地緣政治的圖景異常吻合耶利米的描寫：以色列同時面對伊朗的飛彈、真主黨的火箭、哈瑪斯的恐怖、葉門胡塞的襲擊、敘利亞與伊拉克的代理人——再加上西方教會內部的反猶聲浪。四面圍困，正是「Tzar」的字面意涵。 Rabbi Shapira observes that today's geopolitics uncannily matches Jeremiah's picture: Israel facing Iran's missiles, Hezbollah's rockets, Hamas's terror, Houthi strikes from Yemen, Syrian and Iraqi proxies — plus rising antisemitism from within the Western church itself. Hemmed in on every side — the literal meaning of Tzar.

二、「我們拉比的訓誨第三十六章」── 列王彼此挑釁的米大示II. “Our Rabbi's Teachings, Chapter 36” — A Midrash on Kings Provoking Each Other

拉比沙皮拉引用一段拉比訓誨（他以「我們拉比的訓誨第三十六章」稱之，與我們在第 5 篇看過的 Yalkut Shimoni Remez 499 同一傳統線）所描述的末世順序： Rabbi Shapira cites a rabbinic teaching (he refers to it as “our Rabbi's teachings, Chapter 36” — the same tradition-line as the Yalkut Shimoni Remez 499 we walked through in article 5) describing the end-time sequence:

（1）列國的諸王彼此挑釁。（2）波斯王（伊朗）挑釁阿拉伯王（沙特）。（3）阿拉伯諸王前往以東（美國）尋求建議。（4）「殘忍的波斯王」企圖毀滅整個世界。 (1) The kings of the nations provoke one another. (2) The King of Persia (Iran) provokes an Arab king (Saudi Arabia). (3) The Arab kings go to Edom (America) to seek counsel. (4) The cruel King of Persia attempts to destroy the world.

對任何讀過 2024–2025 年中東新聞的人，這個順序的每一步都不需要詮釋——它就是頭條：伊朗對沙特、阿聯、巴林同時施壓；沙特王儲穆罕默德·本·薩勒曼公開向川普提議對伊朗動武；伊朗領導人公開談論「世界大戰」、「第三次世界大戰」。米大示的劇本，正在被新聞 1:1 重演。 For anyone who has read the Middle East news of 2024–2025, every step requires no interpretation — it is the headline: Iran simultaneously pressing Saudi Arabia, the UAE, and Bahrain; Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman openly advising Trump to strike Iran; Iranian leaders openly speaking of “world war,” “World War III.” The midrash's script is being played out on the news, frame for frame.

三、誰是「以東」？── 美國的角色III. Who is “Edom”? — America's Role

「美國是以東嗎？」這是西方末世論中最有爭議的問題之一。拉比沙皮拉的回答有微妙之處：「美國目前支持以色列，但即將到來的戰爭會在美國國內造成嚴重撕裂與反猶情緒的爆發。」 “Is America Edom?” — one of the most contested questions in Western eschatology. Rabbi Shapira's answer is nuanced: “America presently supports Israel, but the coming war will cause significant internal division and an outbreak of antisemitic sentiment within the US.”

他提出一個極具份量的觀點：川普總統在這場大戲中是「神手中的棋子」(a pawn in God's hand)——不論他自己意識與否，他正在為彌賽亞的降臨清場。但「美國」這個整體既包括親以色列的福音派、也包括日益反以色列的進步派與反猶極右派——這個分裂本身，就是「Edom 內部撕裂」的應驗。 He makes a weighty observation: President Trump in this drama is “a pawn in God's hand” — whether he is conscious of it or not, he is clearing the stage for the Messiah's arrival. But “America” as a whole contains both pro-Israel evangelicals and an increasingly anti-Israel progressive left as well as antisemitic far-right voices. That very split is itself the fulfilment of “Edom's internal tearing.”

四、申命記 4:30 ── Tzarah 之後的歸向IV. Deuteronomy 4:30 — After Tzarah, the Turning

「日後你在患難 (Tzarah) 中，這一切的事都到了你身上，你必歸回耶和華你的神，聽從他的話。耶和華你神原是有憐憫的神；他總不撇下你，不滅絕你，也不忘記他起誓與你列祖所立的約。」——申命記 4:30-31 “When you are in distress (Tzarah), and all these things have come upon you in the latter days, you will return to the LORD your God and obey His voice. For the LORD your God is a merciful God. He will not leave you or destroy you or forget the covenant with your fathers that He swore to them.”— Deuteronomy 4:30-31

申命記 4:30-31 是末世論中極具盼望的一段經文。它說：「Tzarah」之後接著的，不是滅絕，而是「歸回」(Teshuvah，תשובה)。並且這個歸回是因為神記得祂與列祖所立的約——亞伯拉罕之約、以撒之約、雅各之約——這些約是永不撕毀的。 Deuteronomy 4:30–31 is one of the most hopeful passages in eschatology. It says: after Tzarah comes not annihilation but return (Teshuvah, תשובה). And this return happens because God remembers the covenant He swore to the fathers — Abraham's, Isaac's, Jacob's — covenants that are never torn up.

拉比沙皮拉的應用：當我們今天看見以色列的患難、看見全球反猶聲浪、看見教會內部的分裂時，我們應該同時看見：「歸回」的時刻已在門口。不是末日的恐慌，而是末日的覺醒。 Rabbi Shapira's application: as we see today's distress on Israel, the rising tide of global antisemitism, the splits within the Church, we should simultaneously see: the moment of “return” is at the door. Not end-time panic — end-time awakening.

五、Tzar 與 Tzarah ── 字根的雙面意義V. Tzar and Tzarah — The Double Meaning of the Root

Tzarah（צרה，「患難」）的字根 Tzar（צר）有兩面意義：（1）窄、圍困——苦難所感受到的「被擠壓」；（2）磐石、堅實——當被圍困到極致時所立足之處。 The root Tzar (צר) behind Tzarah (צרה, “distress”) has two faces: (1) narrow, besieging — the “squeeze” of suffering; (2) rock, firm — what one stands on when the besieging is at its peak.

詩篇 18:2：「耶和華是我的磐石 (Tzur，צור)，我的山寨，我的救主。」當以色列在 Tzarah（患難）中無路可走時，神自己成為 Tzur（磐石）。同一個字根，從「圍困」變成「立足」。這是希伯來語在末世論中所給的禮物——苦難中藏著磐石。 Psalm 18:2: “The LORD is my rock (Tzur, צור), my fortress, my deliverer.” When Israel runs out of options in Tzarah, God Himself becomes the Tzur. Same root — from “besieging” to “foothold.” That is Hebrew's gift in eschatology: the rock is hidden inside the distress.

六、全球性的覺醒 ── 大歸正的記號VI. A Global Awakening — The Sign of the Great Turning

拉比沙皮拉以一個極具分量的預測作為結尾：末世最大的記號之一，將是「列國的大歸向」——大規模的人從各種背景轉向亞伯拉罕、以撒、雅各的神。我們今天已經看到這個記號的初步形態：在伊朗，年輕一代秘密轉向基督的人數激增；在中國，福音的傳揚速度超過任何時代；在拉丁美洲、非洲、東南亞，「希伯來根源」的教導吸引大量基督徒重新檢視自己的信仰。 Rabbi Shapira closes with a weighty prediction: one of the great signs of the end will be a massive turning of the nations — people from every background turning to the God of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob. We are already seeing the first shape of this sign: in Iran, the younger generation is secretly turning to Christ in surging numbers; in China, the gospel is spreading faster than at any time in history; across Latin America, Africa, and Southeast Asia, “Hebrew-roots” teaching is drawing huge numbers of Christians back to re-examine their faith.

這就是「Tzarah → Teshuvah」的循環在當代被打開的圖景。撒旦的軍隊與神的軍隊正在同時擴大；不存在中立地帶。每個信徒、每個教會、每個民族——都被推到一個必須選邊的時刻。 This is the “Tzarah → Teshuvah” cycle reopening in our day. Satan's armies and God's armies are expanding simultaneously; there is no neutral ground. Every believer, every church, every nation is being pushed to a moment of choosing sides.

七、對華人基督徒的意義VII. What This Means for Chinese Christians

第一，華人教會在過去 30 年的快速增長並不是偶然——它是「列國大歸向」的具體表現之一。每一位走進教會的華人、每一位接受耶書亞為彌賽亞的華人、每一位學習希伯來根源的華人——都站在這個末世圖景的中心。我們不是觀察者，我們是應驗者。 First, the rapid growth of the Chinese church over the past 30 years is no accident — it is one concrete expression of “the great turning of the nations.” Every Chinese believer who walks into a church, every Chinese who accepts Yeshua as Messiah, every Chinese who studies Hebrew roots — stands at the centre of this end-time picture. We are not observers; we are fulfillers.

第二，「Tzarah → Teshuvah」這個循環應該重新校準我們對「中國的處境」的看法。教會在中國所經歷的限制、在西方所經歷的反彈、在公眾論述中所被誤解——這些都是 Tzar（窄、圍困）。但按申命記 4:30 的應許：在這個 Tzarah 之中，歸向的契機正在被打開。 Second, the “Tzarah → Teshuvah” cycle should re-calibrate how we read “China's situation.” The restrictions the Church faces in China, the pushback in the West, the misunderstanding in public discourse — these are all Tzar (narrow, besieging). But by the promise of Deuteronomy 4:30: inside that Tzarah, the moment of return is being opened.

第三，「神是我的磐石 (Tzur)」這個自我宣告，當在 Tzarah 中被反覆說出時，能夠改變一個信徒、一個家庭、一個教會的整個立足點。當你被「圍困」時，記住：同一個字根，也意味著「磐石」。 Third, declaring “the LORD is my Rock (Tzur)” repeatedly during a season of Tzarah can shift the whole footing of a believer, a family, a church. When you are besieged, remember: the same root also means “rock.”

八、關於分辨VIII. A Word on Discernment

拉比沙皮拉所引用的「我們拉比的訓誨第三十六章」並未明確指出原文出處——這是一段在拉比口傳傳統中常被引用、但難以精確定位的文本。對於認真的讀者，建議將這段教導與 Yalkut Shimoni 499 並列閱讀（參考本系列第 5 篇文章），把兩段米大示放在一起，所形成的圖景才完整。 The “our Rabbi's teachings, Chapter 36” that Rabbi Shapira cites is not given a precise source — it is a text often quoted in oral rabbinic tradition but harder to locate exactly. For the serious reader, we suggest reading this teaching alongside Yalkut Shimoni Remez 499 (covered in article 5 of this series); the two midrashim together form the fuller picture.

關於「川普是神手中的棋子」這個應用——它是一個觀察性的判斷，而非聖經明文。歷史中神確實使用了「外邦的君王」（如波斯的居魯士，以賽亞 45:1 稱他為神的「受膏者」）來成就祂對以色列的計劃。但把這個原則應用到當代任何具體的政治人物身上，需要謙卑與分辨——歷史會作出最終的判斷。 On “Trump is a pawn in God's hand” — this is an observational judgement, not plain scripture. God in history has indeed used “Gentile kings” (Cyrus of Persia, whom Isaiah 45:1 calls God's “anointed”) to accomplish His plan for Israel. But applying this principle to any specific contemporary political figure requires humility and discernment — history will render the final verdict.

九、結語 ── 在 Tzarah 中站立IX. Closing — Stand Firm in the Tzarah

「我們已進入雅各的患難 (Tzarat Yaakov) 的最後階段。但記得：申命記 4:30 不是預言滅亡——它預言歸向。神是我的磐石 (Tzur)，在患難 (Tzarah) 之中。願列國大規模歸向亞伯拉罕、以撒、雅各的神。願彌賽亞速速回來。Am Yisrael Chai。」 “We have entered the final phase of Jacob's Trouble (Tzarat Yaakov). But remember: Deuteronomy 4:30 is not a prophecy of annihilation — it is a prophecy of return. The LORD is my Rock (Tzur) in the very midst of distress (Tzarah). May the nations turn in great numbers to the God of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob. May the Messiah come quickly. Am Yisrael Chai.”
