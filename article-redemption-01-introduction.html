救贖之書 · 第一課：導論 The Book of Redemption · Lesson 1: Introduction

沙皮拉拉比博士展開一段橫跨數月、逐字逐句的研讀──研究中世紀偉大拉比拉姆班 (Ramban / Nachmanides) 的《救贖之書》(סֵפֶר הַגְּאוּלָה Sefer HaGeulah)。第一課先認識這位作者，並為這趟關於 גְּאוּלָה geulah（救贖）與彌賽亞降臨的旅程預備我們的心。 Rabbi Dr. Shapira begins a months-long, word-by-word study of Sefer HaGeulah (סֵפֶר הַגְּאוּלָה), the Book of Redemption, by the great medieval sage Ramban (Nachmanides). Lesson One introduces the author and prepares our hearts for a journey into geulah (גְּאוּלָה, redemption) and the coming of the Mashiach.

沙皮拉拉比博士 · Ahavat Ammi 事工 Rabbi Dr. Itzhak Shapira · Ahavat Ammi Ministries
2026 · 5 · 25 · 約 9 分鐘閱讀~9 min read · ▶ 觀看 · 第一課：導論Watch · Lesson 1: Introduction ·

「我教導的熱情之一，是為גְּאוּלָה geulah（救贖）、為彌賽亞 (מָשִׁיחַ Mashiach) 的降臨帶來清晰。」沙皮拉拉比這樣開場。在這第一課裡，他展開一項橫跨數月、逐字逐句的研讀計劃──研究中世紀偉大拉比拉姆班 (רַמְבַּ״ן Ramban，即 Nachmanides) 的《救贖之書》(סֵפֶר הַגְּאוּלָה Sefer HaGeulah)。他坦言：「我並不同意拉姆班所說的一切，但我極深地尊重這部作品。」這趟旅程要問的，是我們的先賢如何看見救贖、如何催促彌賽亞的降臨。 “One of my passions in teaching,” Rabbi Shapira begins, “is to bring clarity for the geulah (גְּאוּלָה, the redemption), the coming of the Mashiach (מָשִׁיחַ).” In this first lesson he opens a months-long, word-by-word study of Sefer HaGeulah (סֵפֶר הַגְּאוּלָה, the Book of Redemption) by the great medieval sage Ramban (רַמְבַּ״ן, Nachmanides). He is candid: “I don't agree with everything Nachmanides says, but I do respect greatly this work.” The journey asks how our sages saw the redemption — and how we might hasten the coming of the Mashiach.

一、為何研讀《救贖之書》I. Why Study the Book of Redemption

沙皮拉拉比說，他教導的熱情之一，就是為救贖、為彌賽亞的降臨帶來清晰──「好叫我們知道神此刻對我們的期待，好叫我們能一同預備道路，迎接彌賽亞的降臨。」 One of Rabbi Shapira's passions in teaching, he says, is to bring clarity for the redemption and the coming of the Mashiach — “so that we know what God expects from us right now, so we can work together to prepare our way for the coming of the Mashiach.”

他對歷代先賢有一句既尊敬又誠實的評語：「我們的先賢，大體而言，都跑了一場美好的賽程。在某些情況下，他們確實看見了救贖清晰的圖畫──然而他們並沒有跑到終點線。」正因如此，他渴望帶來這份特別的教導：逐字逐句地研讀拉姆班的《救贖之書》，「為彌賽亞的身體（信徒群體）帶來對未來、對前路的清晰。」 His verdict on the sages is both reverent and honest: “Our sages, in large, ran a good race. In some cases, they have seen a clear picture of the geulah — yet they did not cross the finish line.” That is precisely why he longs to offer this special teaching: a word-by-word, verse-by-verse study of Ramban's Sefer HaGeulah, “to bring clarity to the body of Messiah on the future and what lies ahead of us.”

他先說了一句免責聲明：「我並不同意拉姆班所說的一切，但我極深地尊重這部作品。」這項研讀要花上許多週、許多月，逐節推進，需要極大的努力與規劃──而他們計劃免費提供。「如果你被這項研讀祝福，請考慮固定與我們同工，好讓我們能繼續製作、並完全免費地把這種品質的內容給你。」 He offers a disclaimer up front: “I don't agree with everything Nachmanides says, but I do respect greatly this work.” The study will take many weeks and months, advancing verse by verse, demanding much effort and planning — and they plan to make it available for free. “If you are blessed by this study, please consider partnering with us regularly so that we can continue to produce and give you this type of quality programming completely free of charge.”

二、拉姆班是誰？── 摩西‧本‧納赫曼II. Who Was the Ramban? — Moshe ben Nachman

在進入《救贖之書》之前，沙皮拉拉比先介紹拉比摩西‧本‧納赫曼 (Rabbi Moshe ben Nachman)，又名納赫曼尼德 (Nachmanides)。他約於 1194 年生於西班牙（一一〇〇年代末期），1270 年逝於以色列地，享年七十四歲（有說七十五歲）。 Before entering Sefer HaGeulah, Rabbi Shapira introduces Rabbi Moshe ben Nachman, also known as Nachmanides. He was born around 1194 in Spain (late 1100s) and died in 1270 in the Land of Israel, at the age of seventy-four — some say seventy-five.

拉姆班有何特別之處？他是中世紀最偉大的拉比之一，是一位擁有哈拉哈（律法）權柄的釋經者──一位 posek（具有作出宗教裁定之法律權威的人）。他同時是醫生、是詩人，在眾多領域都有所拓展。他生於西班牙東北部今日所謂的加泰隆尼亞 (Catalonia) 地區，正值我們所稱的「黃金時代」(Torah Zahav)──里雄尼姆 (Rishonim，早期權威拉比) 的黃金年代，與他同時代的還有伊本‧以斯拉 (Ibn Ezra)、拉什 (Rashi) 與拉姆巴姆 (Rambam，邁蒙尼德) 等大拉比。 What is so special about the Ramban? He was one of the greatest rabbis of the Middle Ages — a commentator with halachic authority, a posek, one with the legal authority to make religious rulings. He was also a physician, a poet, expansive across many fields. He was born in the region of Catalonia in today's northeast Spain, during what is called the golden era (Torah Zahav) of the Rishonim — the era that produced such giants as Ibn Ezra, Rashi, and the Rambam (Maimonides).

沙皮拉拉比把拉姆班與拉姆巴姆並列，視為最偉大者之一：「他的書影響猶太人的思想、猶太人的世界，直到今天。」他的門生拉什巴 (Rashba) 為他作見證說：「在智慧上、在敬畏犯罪上，無人比他更偉大。」拉姆班在許多領域都留下影響──他的詩歌、他培育門生的方式、他釋經的方法、他那革命性的新進路──全都影響至今，「影響我與我的教導，最終也影響你們這些觀看我節目的人。」 Rabbi Shapira places the Ramban alongside the Rambam among the very greatest: “His book affects Jewish thinking, the Jewish world, up to today.” His disciple the Rashba testified of him: “There was none greater than him in wisdom and in fear of sinning.” The Ramban left his mark in many areas — his poetry, his raising of disciples, his method of interpreting the text, his revolutionary new approach — all of which “have an effect even on us today, an effect on me and my teaching, which ultimately affects those of you who watch my programming.”

三、生於兩個世界之間III. Living Between Two Worlds

拉姆班在西班牙活動的地域，正介於基督教的西班牙與穆斯林的西班牙之間。沙皮拉拉比指出：「你可以在他的著作中看出，他常常反映出他在穆斯林世界與基督教世界中所面對的事物」──在他的時代，逼迫顯然正在升高。 The Ramban operated in a part of Spain that sat between the Christian side and the Muslim side. As Rabbi Shapira observes, “You can tell in his writing that many times he reflects some of the things that he dealt with in the Muslim world and the Christian world” — in his day, persecution was clearly on the rise.

正是在這種張力之中，拉姆班成就了一件少有人能成就的事：他把西班牙系 (Sephardic) 與德系 (Ashkenazic) 兩種釋經方法，融合成屬於他自己、獨一無二的風格。沙皮拉拉比說，這種融合的能力，正是拉姆班何以如此與眾不同的原因之一。 It was within that tension that the Ramban accomplished something few could: he managed to combine the Sephardic and the Ashkenazic methods of interpreting the text into his own unique blend. That very ability to fuse two worlds, Rabbi Shapira notes, is part of what makes the Ramban so distinctive.

四、拉姆班的著作與釋經法IV. The Ramban's Works and Method

拉姆班年輕時就已寫成《約伯記》註釋。他後來最偉大的作品，是他對妥拉（摩西五經）的註釋；此外還有《雅歌》註釋、《以賽亞書》第六十三章的註釋，以及大量其他著作──有人說他寫了超過一百四十部不同的文獻，有些留存至今，有些已經佚失。但他最為人知的，仍是他的妥拉註釋。 In his youth the Ramban already wrote a commentary on the book of Job. His greatest work would be his commentary on the Torah, the five books of Moses; he also wrote on the Song of Songs, on Isaiah 63, and an enormous body of other work — some say over 140 different pieces of literature, some of which we still hold, and some of which are lost. Yet his claim to fame remains his commentary on the Torah.

與拉什的孫子拉什巴姆 (Rashbam) 不同──拉什巴姆對一節經文常只寫一兩句話──拉姆班以「冗長」著稱。沙皮拉拉比說，許多時候別的註釋家寫兩三個字，拉姆班卻寫兩三頁。 Unlike Rashi's grandson the Rashbam, who would write a sentence or two about a verse, the Ramban is famous for being lengthy. Many times, Rabbi Shapira says, where other commentators wrote two or three words, the Ramban wrote two or three pages.

他釋經方法的獨特之處，在於他首先從p'shat（字面意義）入手。他是一位語言學家，對聖言（希伯來文）的掌握極為精湛；他對米大示 (midrash) 的理解也極深。他的方法與拉什相似──把米大示與字面義連結起來──但有所不同：拉什往往只是把米大示「拋給」你，拉姆班卻更專注於把p'shat（字面）連回到米大示。 The uniqueness of his method lies in first grasping the p'shat (the literal sense). He was a linguist, with excellent command of the holy tongue, and he understood the midrash in profound depth. His method, similar to Rashi's, was to connect the midrash to the literal — yet where Rashi would simply throw the midrash at you, the Ramban was far more focused on connecting the p'shat back into the midrash.

拉姆班常常引用拉什、伊本‧以斯拉與拉姆巴姆，但他的哲學立場與拉姆巴姆截然不同──拉姆巴姆在許多方面是一位理性主義者，而拉姆班多次與他針鋒相對。例如在《創世記》第十八章、論到探訪亞伯拉罕的三位天使 (malachim) 時，拉姆班猛烈地反駁拉姆巴姆。不過，沙皮拉拉比說，拉姆班對拉什始終極為尊重；對同時代另一位大註釋家伊本‧以斯拉，則多了幾分批判。 The Ramban often quoted Rashi, Ibn Ezra, and the Rambam, but his philosophical view differed sharply from the Rambam, who was in many ways a rationalist — and the Ramban repeatedly “got in his face.” In his commentary on Genesis 18, on the three malachim (angels) who visited Avraham Avinu, the Ramban attacked the Rambam ferociously. Yet, Rabbi Shapira notes, the Ramban always treated Rashi with great respect; toward Ibn Ezra, the other great commentator of the same era, he was somewhat more critical.

與拉姆巴姆不同，拉姆班大量運用了一些卡巴拉 (Kabbalah)、神祕主義猶太教的成分。他是第一位把完整的 PaRDeS 釋經法帶入今日所知用法的人──這套方法說到理解經文的四個層次：p'shat（字面）、remez（暗示）、drash（米大示式的探求）、sod（奧祕）。這四個層次本就見於先賢 (Chazal) 之中，但正是拉姆班把它們重新整合，帶回猶太釋經。 Unlike the Rambam, the Ramban made much use of Kabbalah, of mystical Judaism. He was the first to bring the full PaRDeS into usage as we know it — the four levels of understanding the text: p'shat (the literal), remez (the hint), drash (the midrashic search), and sod (the secret). The four layers were already found in Chazal, but it was the Ramban who brought them back together into Jewish commentary.

五、巴塞隆納辯論V. The Disputation of Barcelona

拉姆班也持續對抗基督徒與天主教。在西班牙王雅各一世 (Jacob the First) 強迫猶太人改宗之後，拉姆班參與了著名的「巴塞隆納辯論」(Disputation of Barcelona)──一場極其著名、公開的辯論，對手是一位改宗天主教的猶太人，名叫保羅‧克里斯蒂亞尼 (Paulus Christiani)。 The Ramban also continued to contend against the Christians and Catholics. After King Jacob the First of Spain forced Jewish people to convert, the Ramban took part in the famous Disputation of Barcelona — a very public debate against a Jew who had converted to Catholicism, named Paulus Christiani.

這場辯論收錄在拉姆班的著作《辯論之序》(Sefer HaVikuach) 中，整場辯論都可閱讀。沙皮拉拉比指出，辯論的核心議題正是彌賽亞的身分 (Messiahship)、彌賽亞的神性，以及三位一體 (the Trinity)。這場辯論最終為拉姆班帶來極大的成功：王賜給他三百枚錢幣，並告訴他這是他所應得的。 The debate is preserved in the Ramban's book Sefer HaVikuach, where the entire exchange can be read. Rabbi Shapira notes that its central topics were precisely the Messiahship, the divinity of the Mashiach, and the Trinity. The disputation ended in great success for the Ramban: the king gave him 300 coins and told him it was what he deserved.

六、《救贖之書》前方的問題VI. The Questions Ahead in Sefer HaGeulah

在《救贖之書》的引言中，一些註釋家寫道：救贖之事「在以色列中是已知的──我們的心向著彌賽亞的降臨甦醒」；然而它同時是隱藏的、是奧祕。它不只是 geulah──「Ela gam eur pal vechashuch」──其中還有黑暗與祕密。因為我們的拉比說，這一場 גָּלוּת galut（流亡），就是「羅馬的流亡」(Galut Romi)。 In the introduction to Sefer HaGeulah, some commentators wrote that the redemption “is known in Israel — our heart is awakening to the coming of the Mashiach”; yet it is also hidden, a mystery. It is not only geulah — “ela gam eur pal vechashuch” — there is darkness and secret in it. For our rabbis say that this galut (גָּלוּת, our present exile) is like a “Galut Romi,” the exile of Rome.

由此引出一連串拉姆班要探討的問題，也正是這套研讀要面對的問題：羅馬是誰？羅馬代表什麼？以實瑪利 (Ishmael) 是誰、代表誰？約瑟之子彌賽亞 (Messiah son of Yosef) 是誰？大衛之子彌賽亞 (Messiah son of David) 又是誰？失落的十支派在哪裡、在這當中扮演什麼角色？以賽亞書第九章說的是什麼？gematria（字母數值法）如何運用？「妥拉的劍」是什麼？救贖向列國的啟示又是什麼？ From this flows the cascade of questions the Ramban takes up — and that this study will face: Who is Rome, and what does Rome represent? Who is Ishmael, and whom does he represent? Who is the Messiah son of Yosef? Who is the Messiah son of David? Where are the ten lost tribes, and what part do they play? What of Isaiah chapter 9? What of the use of gematria? What of “the Sword of the Torah”? What of the revelation of the redemption to the nations?

沙皮拉拉比說，這些都是我們的百姓至今仍在尋求答案的問題。「在《救贖之書》裡，我們將要找到的答案，不只是如何研讀，更是如何催促彌賽亞的降臨──與拉姆班一同尋找。」他鼓勵聽眾：「所以坐穩了，我鼓勵你拿出筆記本，預備你的心，迎接一段特別的旅程。」 These, Rabbi Shapira says, are questions our people are still seeking to answer. “In Sefer HaGeulah we are going to find answers not just how to study, but how to hasten the coming of the Mashiach — together with the Nachmanides.” He urges his listeners: “So sit tight; I encourage you to get a notebook and prepare your heart for a special journey.”

七、拉姆班的禱告 ── 一段結語VII. The Ramban's Prayer — A Closing

沙皮拉拉比以拉姆班自己的話、自己的禱告作為這段引言的結束。拉姆班在《救贖之書》最後一卷中寫道（《救贖之書》是由數本各自獨立的小冊匯成的）： Rabbi Shapira closes the introduction with the Ramban's own words and his own prayer, written as the last booklet within the book — for Sefer HaGeulah is itself gathered from several separate booklets. The Ramban wrote:

「Yehi ratzon──願這成為祢的旨意，lifnei Adonai Eloheinu ve'Elohei avoteinu──在耶和華我們的神、我們列祖的神、宇宙的主宰面前：sheyachish geulateinu──願祂催促我們的救贖；vayomar dai letzaroteinu──願祂說『夠了，我百姓的患難』。因為唯獨祂是Elokei haChesed va'Arachamim──滿有慈愛與恩典的神。」──拉姆班，《救贖之書》 “Yehi ratzon — may it be Your will, lifnei Adonai Eloheinu ve'Elohei avoteinu — before the LORD our God and the God of our fathers, Master of the Universe: sheyachish geulateinu, that He hasten our redemption; vayomar dai letzaroteinu, and say, ‘Enough for our tribulation.’ For He alone is Elokei haChesed va'Arachamim, the God of mercy and grace.”— Ramban, Sefer HaGeulah

拉姆班接著寫道：「沒有任何事能攔阻祂的旨意──沒有日期、沒有時刻。祂是時間的作者。」沙皮拉拉比把這段禱告化作對我們的呼求：願祂引導我們行祂的工；願我們在祂的妥拉中看見清晰的光；願我們經歷神蹟與奇事；願這是一個行神蹟的時節。阿們。 The Ramban continues: “There is nothing that stands before His will — no dates and no times. He is the author of the times.” Rabbi Shapira turns the prayer toward us: May He guide us to do His work; may we see a clear light in His Torah and His great mercy; may we experience a sign and a wonder; may it be a season of miracles. Amen.

「這就是我為我們所獻上的禱告，」沙皮拉拉比說。「願我們一同編織的這份《救贖之書》研讀，成為你超自然的學習，使你愈發親近認識亞伯拉罕、以撒、雅各的神。」 “That is my prayer for us,” Rabbi Shapira says. “May this study that we put together on Sefer HaGeulah be a supernatural study to you, as you come closer to know the God of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.”

「願祂催促我們的救贖，並說：『夠了，我百姓的患難』──因為唯獨祂是滿有慈愛與憐憫的神。沒有任何事攔阻祂的旨意；祂是時間的作者。願這成為一個行神蹟的時節。阿們。」 “May He hasten our redemption and say, ‘Enough for our tribulation’ — for He alone is the God of mercy and grace. Nothing stands before His will; He is the author of the times. May it be a season of miracles. Amen.”
