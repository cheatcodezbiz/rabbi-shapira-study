神的國近了 The Kingdom of G-D Is at Hand

在新年的曙光裡，耶書亞踏入歷史，宣告：「悔改吧，因為天國近了。」這國度由一位永遠掌權的君王統管；它像那塊「非人手鑿出的石頭」，必擊碎一切屬世的國。你和我，是這位萬王之王的大使——被接進以色列，與祂一同預備那即將顯現的國度。 In the dawn of a new year, Yeshua steps onto the stage of history and proclaims: “Repent, for the kingdom of heaven is at hand.” This kingdom is led by a King who reigns forever; it is the “stone cut without hands” that crushes every earthly kingdom. You and I are ambassadors of the King of Kings — grafted into Israel, preparing with Him for the kingdom about to appear.

Nyatsanza 牧師 · Ahavat Ammi 事工 Pastor Nyatsanza · Ahavat Ammi Ministries
2026 · 5 · 25 · 约 7 分钟阅读~7 min read · ▶ 觀看 · 神的國近了Watch · The Kingdom of G-D Is at Hand ·

在新年的曙光裡，Nyatsanza 牧師站在辛巴威——一個按字母排序總是排在「名單末端」的國度——向全地發出一個古老卻又嶄新的宣告：「悔改吧，因為神的國近了。」這正是耶書亞 (Yeshua) 在馬太福音第四章踏入加利利、走進迦百農時所宣告的第一句話。牧師帶我們從馬太福音回到但以理書，再到以賽亞書、申命記與啟示錄，重新看見那塊「非人手鑿出的石頭」——這石頭就是那位永遠掌權的君王，祂必擊碎一切屬世的國，建立一個永不毀壞的國度。問題只有一個：當君王像夜間的賊一樣突然來到時，你的燈裡還有油嗎？ In the dawn of a new year, Pastor Nyatsanza stands in Zimbabwe — a nation that, when you count by the alphabet, always lands near the end of the list — and sends out across the whole earth a proclamation that is ancient and brand-new: “Repent, for the kingdom of G-D is at hand.” These were the very first words Yeshua proclaimed in Matthew 4 as He entered Galilee and settled in Capernaum. The pastor walks us from Matthew back into Daniel, and on through Isaiah, Deuteronomy, and Revelation, to see again the “stone cut out without hands” — the stone that is the King who reigns forever, who crushes every earthly kingdom and establishes one that can never be destroyed. The question is only this: when the King comes suddenly, like a thief in the night, will there still be oil in your lamp?

一、新年的宣告 ── 神的國近了I. A New-Year Proclamation — The Kingdom Is at Hand

Nyatsanza 牧師以「Shana Tova」（שָׁנָה טוֹבָה，「新年蒙福」）向眾人問安，並說這篇信息在他心中翻騰已有數月。在這嶄新的一年裡，他要我們專注思想一件事——「神的國近了」(the kingdom of G-D is at hand)。他從馬太福音 4:12 起讀：當耶書亞聽見約翰被下在監裡，就退到加利利，離開拿撒勒，來到迦百農住下。 Pastor Nyatsanza greets everyone with “Shana Tova” (שָׁנָה טוֹבָה, “a good year”) and says this word has stirred his heart for months. In this brand-new year he wants us to fix our minds on one thing — the kingdom of G-D that is at hand. He reads from Matthew 4:12: when Yeshua heard that John had been handed over, He withdrew to Galilee, left Nazareth, and settled in Capernaum.

「那坐在黑暗裡的百姓看見了大光；坐在死蔭之地的人有光發現照著他們。」——馬太福音 4:16（引以賽亞書 9:1-2） “The people sitting in darkness have seen a great light, and those sitting in the region and shadow of death — on them a light has dawned.”— Matthew 4:16 (quoting Isaiah 9:1–2)

從那時候起，耶書亞就開始傳道，說：「悔改吧，因為天國近了」(馬太福音 4:17)。「悔改」在希伯來思想中是 תְּשׁוּבָה teshuvah——意思是「回轉」，轉離罪、轉向神。「近了」則是 קָרוֹב karov——不是遙遠的將來，而是「就在手邊、伸手可及」。牧師強調：耶書亞所做的一切，起點就是給祂所遇見的每一個人——「先是猶太人，後是外邦人」——同一個指示：回轉吧，因為「天國」(מַלְכוּת שָׁמַיִם malchut shamayim) 已經臨近。 From then on Yeshua began to proclaim: “Repent, for the kingdom of heaven is at hand” (Matthew 4:17). “Repent” in Hebrew thought is תְּשׁוּבָה teshuvah — to “turn back,” away from sin and toward G-D. “At hand” is קָרוֹב karov — not a distant future but something “near, within reach.” The pastor stresses: everything Yeshua came to do began with one instruction to everyone He met — “to the Jew first, and then to the Gentile” — turn back, for the kingdom of heaven (מַלְכוּת שָׁמַיִם malchut shamayim) has drawn near.

二、但以理的石頭 ── 非人手鑿出II. Daniel's Stone — Cut Without Hands

這國度由一位君王統管。牧師翻到但以理書 2:44——但以理為尼布甲尼撒王解夢時宣告：「當那列王在位的時候，天上的神必另立一國，永不敗壞，也不歸別國的人，卻要打碎滅絕那一切國，這國必存到永遠。」 This kingdom is led by a King. The pastor turns to Daniel 2:44 — Daniel, interpreting King Nebuchadnezzar's dream, declares: “In the days of those kings, the G-D of heaven will set up a kingdom that will never be destroyed, nor will it be left to another people. It will crush and bring to an end all these kingdoms, but it will endure forever.”

歷世歷代許多教父與學者都認同：但以理所說的列國，依次是巴比倫、波斯、希臘、羅馬；而那塊「非人手鑿出的石頭」——並非靠人的力量、不是靠政治、不是靠刀劍——指向耶書亞的道成肉身。祂登上歷史的舞台，說出同一句話：「悔改吧，因為神的國近了。」這正是我們進入新年時所領受的指示：辭舊迎新，並帶著一份催逼——一份臨到我們每一個人身上的急迫感。 Through the centuries many church fathers and students have agreed: the kingdoms Daniel names are, in order, Babylon, Persia, Greece, and Rome; and the “stone cut out without hands” — not by human power, not by politics, not by the sword — points to the incarnation of Yeshua. He comes onto the scene and says the same word: “Repent, for the kingdom of G-D is at hand.” This is the instruction we receive as we enter a new year: we leave an old year and begin a new one with a holy impetus — an urgency pressing upon every single one of us.

牧師滿心喜樂地說：即使是從「名單末端」的辛巴威，也要發出這宣告、發出這道命令：「悔改吧，因為神的國近了。」這石頭——但以理所見的石頭——要擊碎一切不屬於神的國。凡想要在這國度面前站立的國，都必被毀滅；一個非人手所造、非人手所鑿的國度將要顯現，廢去一切別的國，建立一個全新的國——神的國。 With great joy the pastor says: even from Zimbabwe at the end of the list, we make this proclamation, we issue this decree — “Repent, for the kingdom of G-D is at hand.” This stone — the stone Daniel saw — will crush every kingdom that is not of G-D. Whatever kingdom tries to stand before this kingdom will be destroyed; a kingdom not made with human hands, a stone not cut by human hands, will appear and abolish every other kingdom, establishing a new one — the kingdom of G-D.

三、被棄的石頭成了房角石III. The Rejected Stone Becomes the Cornerstone

這塊石頭並不陌生——它貫穿整本聖經。耶書亞自己在馬太福音 21:42 引用詩篇：「匠人所棄的石頭，已成了房角的頭塊石頭。」而以賽亞書 28:16 早已預言這塊石頭—— This stone is not unfamiliar — it runs through the whole of Scripture. Yeshua Himself quotes the Psalm in Matthew 21:42: “The stone which the builders rejected has become the cornerstone.” And Isaiah 28:16 had long before foretold this stone —

「主耶和華如此說：看哪，我在錫安放一塊石頭作為根基，是試驗過的石頭，是穩固根基，寶貴的房角石；信靠的人必不著急。」——以賽亞書 28:16 “Thus says Adonai Elohim: Behold, I am laying in Zion a stone, a tested stone, a costly cornerstone, a firm foundation. Whoever trusts in it will not flee in haste or be afraid.”— Isaiah 28:16

牧師把這幾處經文連成一線：但以理所說的那塊石頭、新約裡所見的那塊石頭、耶書亞親口所說「匠人所棄」如今成了房角石的那塊石頭——都是同一塊石頭。這塊石頭，按但以理所言，要擊碎一切不屬神的國。這是何等的安慰與喜樂的話語，在新年之初臨到我們：凡敵擋這國度的，都必傾倒。 The pastor draws these texts into one line: the stone Daniel spoke of, the stone we see in the New Testament, the very stone Yeshua names — “the one the builders rejected,” now become the cornerstone — are all the same stone. And this stone, Daniel says, will crush every kingdom that is not of G-D. What a word of encouragement and joy at the start of a new year: whatever stands against this kingdom will fall.

四、永遠掌權的君王IV. The King Who Reigns Forever

這國度有一位君王，而我們今天正在「慶賀祂的加冕」。牧師宣告，這位君王永遠作王，正如出埃及記 15:18 所說：「耶和華必作王，直到永永遠遠。」申命記 33:5 描述祂「在耶書崙中為王」；而以色列信仰的核心——示瑪 (Shema)——在申命記 6:4 宣告：「以色列啊，你要聽！耶和華我們神是獨一的主。」 This kingdom has a King, and today we are “enjoying His coronation.” The pastor proclaims that this King reigns forever, as Exodus 15:18 says: “The LORD will reign forever and ever.” Deuteronomy 33:5 calls Him King in Jeshurun; and the heart of Israel's faith — the Shema — declares in Deuteronomy 6:4: “Hear, O Israel: the LORD is our G-D, the LORD alone.”

牧師描繪一幅動人的圖畫：外邦人與猶太人一同站在一處，齊聲宣告這位君王是萬王之王。這位君王施行公義，永遠掌權，披戴威嚴，以能力束腰；祂所立的牆堅立不可動搖。以賽亞書 44:6 為這一切作結：「耶和華以色列的君，以色列的救贖主萬軍之耶和華如此說：我是首先的，我是末後的；除我以外再沒有真神。」 The pastor paints a moving picture: Gentiles and Jews standing together in one place, proclaiming as one that this King is the King of Kings. This King dispenses justice, reigns forever, is robed in grandeur, girded with strength; the wall He establishes stands firm and cannot be shaken. Isaiah 44:6 seals it all: “Thus says the LORD, the King of Israel and his Redeemer, the LORD of hosts: I am the first and I am the last; and besides Me there is no G-D.”

五、被接進來的大使V. Ambassadors Grafted In

牧師談到耶路撒冷今日的光景：穆斯林、天主教徒、聖公會信徒、東正教徒、猶太人——眾人都聚集在這一處。他說，這並非偶然，而是一個記號與奇事：因為萬王之王將要興起，奪回祂的位置，掌權管理一切別的國。而你和我，就是這位君王的大使。「光是想到我代表萬王之王，就讓我覺得自己何等尊貴。」 The pastor describes Jerusalem as it is today: Muslims, Catholics, Anglicans, the Orthodox, Jews — all of them gathered in this one place. This, he says, is no mistake but a sign and a wonder: because the King of Kings is going to rise up, take His place, and take dominion over every other kingdom. And you and I are the ambassadors of that King. “It makes me feel special that I represent the King of Kings.”

牧師引述 Matt 牧師的教導——外邦信徒是被接進以色列的（參羅馬書 11）。正因為我們被嫁接進以色列，凡賜給以色列的份，我們也一同有份。所以我們說「Shana Tova，甜蜜的新年」：給以色列的應許，也是從非洲、從這地、從每一個被接進來的人所領受的應許。我們起來宣告：我們已被接進以色列，要與萬王之王一同作王，站在祂的威嚴中，行在祂的尊榮裡，在這時代、這季節堅立祂的國。 The pastor cites Pastor Matt's teaching — Gentile believers are grafted into Israel (cf. Romans 11). Because we are grafted in, whatever portion is given to Israel, we partake of it too. So we say, “Shana Tova, a sweet new year”: the promise made to Israel is the promise made to us — from Africa, from this place, from everyone grafted in. We rise and declare: we have been grafted into Israel to reign with the King of Kings, to stand in His majesty, to walk in His grandeur, and to establish His kingdom in this season, in this time.

「我們或許肉眼看不見，耳朵也聽不到，但在我們心裡已經刻著：神的國近了。」但以理所見的那塊石頭是活的，今日已經堅立。這石頭就是我們眾人所等候的萬王之王——就是那位曾行走在地上、宣告「神的國近了」的同一位君王。祂的使命不僅是醫病，不僅是餵飽千萬人，更是要對以色列說：時候到了，你們當明白——君王正在建立祂的國。 “We may not see it with our eyes, we may not hear it with our ears, but in our hearts it is inscribed: the kingdom of G-D is at hand.” The stone Daniel saw is alive and established today. This stone is the King of Kings we are all waiting for — the same King who walked on the earth and proclaimed, “the kingdom of G-D is at hand.” His mission was not only to heal, not only to feed thousands, but to say to Israel: the time has come for you to understand that the King is establishing His kingdom.

六、燈裡的油 ── 預備迎接君王VI. Oil in the Lamp — Be Ready for the King

這國度像一塊磐石，必擊碎一切屬地的國。這豈不帶給你安慰？豈不叫你心中湧起喜樂，叫你想要舉手向主獻上「Shabbat」，向永恆的君王歌頌？但牧師也發出警告：君王要像夜間的賊一樣來到，祂要在半夜來到。 This kingdom, like a mighty rock, will crush all earthly kingdoms. Does that not bring you comfort? Does it not stir joy in your heart, make you want to raise your hand, give “Shabbat” to the Lord, sing praises to the eternal King? But the pastor also sounds a warning: the King will come like a thief in the night; He will come at midnight.

你和我，是否像那五個聰明的童女一樣，預備了足夠的油？當新郎來到時，她們已預備好與祂同去；她們不被任何別的事分心，不像那些愚拙的童女毫無預備。她們不肯睡著，因為知道祂要來。牧師問：我們當中是否有人正被眼前世界的景況催入沉睡？我們是否漸漸疲乏、開始失去專注、油快用盡了？「我們需要快快把油加滿。」這正是耶書亞向列國所傳的信息：快快加滿你的油，因為君王要來了；祂要分開綿羊與山羊，祂要發出極大的光輝，吞滅一切的黑暗。 Are you and I, like the five wise virgins, supplied with enough oil? When the groom came, they were ready to go with him; they were not distracted by anything else, not unprepared like the others. They would not sleep, because they knew He was coming. The pastor asks: are some of us being lulled to slumber by what we see in our current world? Are we growing tired, losing our concentration, running out of oil? “We need to fill it up quickly.” This is what Yeshua brings to the nations: fill up your oil quickly, for the King is coming; He will separate the sheep from the goats, He will shine so brightly that He swallows up all darkness.

七、末了 ── 神成為萬物之中的萬有VII. The End — G-D All in All

牧師翻到哥林多前書 15:24-26：「再後，末期到了，那時基督既將一切執政的、掌權的、有能的都毀滅了，就把國交與父神。因為基督必要作王，等神把一切仇敵都放在祂的腳下。儘末了所毀滅的仇敵就是死。」 The pastor turns to 1 Corinthians 15:24–26: “Then the end will come, when He hands over the kingdom to G-D the Father, after He has destroyed all dominion, authority, and power. For He must reign until He has put all His enemies under His feet. The last enemy to be destroyed is death.”

而當經上說「萬物」都服了祂，這顯然不包括那叫萬物服祂的神自己。等萬物都服了祂，那時子也要自己服那叫萬物服祂的，叫神在萬物之中作萬有 (that G-D may be all in all)。牧師說：這就是神國的終局。隨後他以宏大的「Aleinu」禱告作結——願神在祂一切的作為中彰顯，願一切受造之物都認識祂。 And when it says “all things” are put under Him, this clearly does not include G-D Himself, who put everything under the Son. When all is subjected, then the Son Himself will be made subject to the One who put everything under Him, so that G-D may be all in all. This, the pastor says, is the end of the kingdom of G-D. He then closes by reciting the great Aleinu prayer — that G-D may be manifest in all His works, and that all creatures may come to know Him.

牧師為「神的同在在世上戲劇性地復興」禱告——這復興要使人心生敬畏，不是帶來分裂，而是帶來全人類的合一：猶太人與外邦人聯合，一同建立神的國，為這國度的堅立預備道路、修直道路。願我們的兒女在同一個團契裡聯合，把耶書亞的權柄帶到全地，從地上除去一切暴政的轄制。「這就是彌賽亞的異象，是我們所跟隨的。」 The pastor prays for “a dramatic revival of G-D's presence in the world” — a revival that instills awe and leads not to division but to the unity of all mankind: Jew and Gentile coming together, establishing the kingdom of G-D, making the way ready for it to be established. May our children unite in one fellowship and bring the dominion of Yeshua over all the earth, removing the dominion of tyranny from the earth. “This is the messianic vision; this is what we follow.”

他以一個呼召作結：我們是為「這樣的時候」而被造的，要在我們所在之處作那盞「燭台」(menorah、מְנוֹרָה)。我們是世上的光，不被藏在桌子底下，而是被放在桌子上，好叫我們照亮萬國，指明方向、指明道路，並帶來秩序——正如創世記第一章，當神看見地上的混沌，祂一說話，秩序就出現了。願我們也照樣，在這嶄新的一年，行在神的腳蹤裡、行在祂的權柄中，為今日的世界帶來秩序。 He ends with a call: we were created for “such a time as this,” to be the menorah (מְנוֹרָה) in the places where we are. We are the light of the world, not hidden under the table but set on top of it, so that we can be a light to all the nations — showing direction, showing the way, and bringing order, just as in Genesis 1, when G-D saw the chaos on the earth, spoke, and there was order. May we walk in His footsteps and in His authority, bringing order to our world in this brand-new year.

「我們或許肉眼看不見，耳朵也聽不到，但在我們的心裡已經刻著：神的國近了。哈利路亞，歸給萬王之王！耶書亞，祢在至高處掌權，我們永永遠遠尊崇祢的聖名。Shana Tova。」 “We may not see it with our eyes, we may not hear it with our ears, but in our hearts it is inscribed: the kingdom of G-D is at hand. Hallelujah to the King of Kings! Yeshua, You reign on high, and we exalt Your holy name forever and ever. Shana Tova.”
