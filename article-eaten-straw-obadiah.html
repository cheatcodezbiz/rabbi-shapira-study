「被吞噬的草芥」── 古蘭經中三個字印證《俄巴底亞書》1:18 “Eaten Straw” — Three Quranic Words That Confirm Obadiah 1:18

真主黨將最近一場攻擊以色列的行動命名為「被吞噬的草芥」(Mebsai Hakash Hanehao)——這個名字源自古蘭經一段末世經文。沙皮拉拉比指出：這個阿拉伯文短語幾乎逐字對應《俄巴底亞書》1:18 中關於以掃家被「燒盡如禾稭」的預言——以色列的敵人正在自己的口中宣告聖經預言的應驗。 Hezbollah named its latest operation against Israel Mebsai Hakash Hanehao — “Eaten Straw” — drawing the name from an eschatological passage of the Quran. Rabbi Shapira's observation: that Arabic phrase is almost a word-for-word match with Obadiah 1:18 on the burning of Esau “as stubble.” Israel's enemies are now naming their own operations after the prophecy of their own end.

沙皮拉拉比博士 · Ahavat Ammi 事工 Rabbi Dr. Itzhak Shapira · Ahavat Ammi Ministries

俄巴底亞書 1:18：「雅各家必成為火，約瑟家必成為火焰；以掃家必如碎稭，火必將他們燒盡，必沒有以掃家的剩餘者。」在希伯來文中，「燒盡如稭」這個動詞短語是 Va'akhalum（וַאֲכָלוּם）——「他們將被吞噬」。真主黨在最近一場行動中所選用的阿拉伯文名字 Fajala hum ka asfin makulin（「他們將像被吞噬的草芥」）幾乎是這節希伯來經文的逐字翻譯。拉比沙皮拉指出：這不是隨機的修辭巧合——這是先知書中所警告的「以掃家如碎稭被燒盡」的應驗，由那些被應驗者自己宣告。 Obadiah 1:18: “The house of Jacob shall be a fire, and the house of Joseph a flame, and the house of Esau stubble; they shall set them ablaze and devour them, and there shall be no remnant of the house of Esau.” In Hebrew, the phrase “burned as stubble” turns on the verb Va'akhalum (וַאֲכָלוּם) — “they shall be devoured.” Hezbollah's chosen Arabic name for its recent operation — Fajala hum ka asfin makulin (“they will be like devoured straw”) — is almost a word-for-word translation of that Hebrew prophecy. Rabbi Shapira's observation: this is not a stylistic coincidence. It is the prophecy of Obadiah being announced by the very ones it announces.

一、真主黨的「被吞噬的草芥」行動I. Hezbollah's “Operation Eaten Straw”

在最近一輪對以色列北部的攻擊中，真主黨給其行動命名為 Mebsai Hakash Hanehao（「被吞噬的草芥」）。這個名字並非隨意取的——它直接出自古蘭經中一段末世經文（《古蘭經》第 105 章「象的章節」），那段經文描寫「象之軍」被神所擊潰，化為「被吞噬的乾草」。 In a recent round of strikes on northern Israel, Hezbollah named its operation Mebsai Hakash Hanehao (“Operation Eaten Straw”). The name was not chosen casually — it is taken from an eschatological Quranic chapter (Surah 105, “The Elephant”), which describes “the army of the elephant” being shattered by God and made “like devoured dry straw.”

對伊斯蘭傳統來說，這段古蘭經文具有強烈的「神戰勝壓迫者」的意涵。真主黨選用這個名字，意圖是說：以色列正在被神「化為草芥」。但拉比沙皮拉指出：他們選錯了句子。 In Islamic tradition the verse carries a strong sense of “God overthrowing the oppressor.” Hezbollah's choice of name was meant to say: Israel is being “made into straw” by God. Rabbi Shapira's observation: they picked the wrong line.

二、阿拉伯文 vs 希伯來文 ── 一個逐字的對應II. Arabic vs Hebrew — A Word-for-Word Match

「以掃家必為碎稭，火必將他們燒盡，必沒有以掃家的剩餘者。」Va'akhalum（וַאֲכָלוּם，「他們將被吞噬」）——俄巴底亞書 1:18 “The house of Esau shall be stubble; they shall set them ablaze and devour them, and no survivor shall be left of the house of Esau.” Va'akhalum (וַאֲכָלוּם, “they shall be devoured”)— Obadiah 1:18

希伯來文 Va'akhalum 與阿拉伯文 Fajala hum ka asfin makulin 在語義上的對應——尤其是「稭」(kash / asfin) 與「被吞噬」(akhalum / makulin) 兩個關鍵字——幾乎完美。希伯來文與阿拉伯文同屬閃語族，許多字根共享。但這個特定組合在兩種語言中同時出現於末世性經文中，意義是顯而易見的。 The Hebrew Va'akhalum and the Arabic Fajala hum ka asfin makulin correspond almost perfectly — especially the two pivots: “stubble” (kash / asfin) and “devoured” (akhalum / makulin). Hebrew and Arabic are sister Semitic languages, sharing many roots. But the particular pairing surfacing in eschatological texts of both traditions makes the resonance unavoidable.

問題的關鍵：俄巴底亞書 1:18 的主語是誰？答案：以掃家。誰是「以掃家」？按古典猶太解經傳統（拉什、阿巴貝爾、拉達克），以掃家在不同時代有不同的歷史化身——羅馬帝國、基督教世界、以及今日的阿拉伯—伊斯蘭聯盟（特別是亞瑪力的後裔）。 The crucial question: who is the subject of Obadiah 1:18? Answer: the house of Esau. Who is “the house of Esau”? In classical Jewish exegesis (Rashi, Abarbanel, Radak), Esau takes different historical incarnations across the ages — the Roman Empire, the Christian nations, and in our day specific Arab-Islamic powers (especially the descendants of Amalek).

三、「雅各家為火、約瑟家為火焰」── 拉達克與拉什的解讀III. “Jacob is Fire, Joseph is Flame” — Radak and Rashi

拉達克（Radak，Rabbi David Kimchi）對俄巴底亞書 1:18 有一段令人震動的注釋：「以掃的後裔最終的覆滅，將藉由約瑟或他的後裔之手實現。」這是一句極具份量的話——它把「以掃的終結」與「約瑟」這條譜系直接連起來。 The Radak (Rabbi David Kimchi) writes a striking comment on Obadiah 1:18: “the descendants of Esau will only meet their final end through the hand of Joseph or his descendants.” A weighty line — it ties “the end of Esau” directly to the line of Joseph.

拉什（Rashi，11 世紀）注意到俄巴底亞書 1:18 末尾的「主如此說 (ki Adonai diber)」——他把這個結尾讀為指向那位將要降臨的彌賽亞君王，那位將親自完成這場以掃覆滅之戰的彌賽亞。 Rashi (11th c.) notes the closing words of Obadiah 1:18 — “for the LORD has spoken” (ki Adonai diber) — and reads them as pointing to the coming Messiah King, the one who will personally bring this destruction of Esau to completion.

四、「約瑟」── 約書亞 / 耶書亞IV. “Joseph” — Joshua / Yeshua

米大示傳統（《Bereishit Rabbah》與其他文集）做出一個對基督徒來說極為重要的識別：俄巴底亞書 1:18 中「約瑟家」所要燒盡以掃家的那「火焰」，就是約書亞 (Yehoshua) ── 那位出自以法蓮、約瑟支派的領袖。 Midrashic tradition (Bereishit Rabbah and others) makes a key identification for any Christian reader: the “flame from the house of Joseph” in Obadiah 1:18 that consumes the stubble of Esau is Joshua (Yehoshua) — the leader from Ephraim, the tribe of Joseph.

約書亞的希伯來名字是 Yehoshua（יהושע）——其縮短形式正是 Yeshua（ישוע），即「耶書亞」（耶穌的希伯來名字）。換言之：拉比傳統中「燒盡以掃的約瑟之火焰」與基督徒所信的耶書亞，共享同一個名字、同一條譜系、同一個角色——擊敗亞瑪力的那一位。 Joshua's full Hebrew name is Yehoshua (יהושע) — and its short form is Yeshua (ישוע), the Hebrew name of Jesus. In other words: the rabbinic tradition's “flame from Joseph that burns up Esau” and the Yeshua Christians worship share one name, one lineage, and one office — the one who defeats Amalek.

這個對應對華人基督徒尤其重要。新約並非把「猶太彌賽亞」改寫成「基督教彌賽亞」——新約是把那位拉比米大示中早就指出的「擊敗亞瑪力的約瑟之火焰」，宣告為已經來、且還要再來的耶書亞。 This is especially important for Chinese Christians. The New Testament does not rewrite the “Jewish Messiah” into a “Christian Messiah.” It announces that the “flame from Joseph who defeats Amalek,” long named in rabbinic midrash, has already come — and will come again — as Yeshua.

五、Gematria ── Kash = 400V. Gematria — Kash = 400

希伯來文「稭」(kash，קש) 的數值是 400（Qof = 100，Shin = 300，Aleph 在某些拼法中加入；經典的 קש = 400）。米大示連到撒母耳記上 30 章——大衛擊敗亞瑪力人時，「沒有一個逃脫，除了 400 個少年人騎著駱駝逃跑」(撒上 30:17)。 The Hebrew word for “stubble” (kash, קש) carries the numerical value 400 (Qof = 100, Shin = 300). Midrash links this to 1 Samuel 30 — when David defeated the Amalekites, “not a man escaped, except four hundred young men who rode camels and fled.” (1 Sam 30:17).

拉比們的解讀：那 400 個逃脫的亞瑪力人，象徵著仍未被處理的邪惡力量——直到末日由彌賽亞親自完成審判。今天「以稭被吞噬」的這個圖景，正在處理的，就是那 400 的剩餘。 The rabbis' reading: those 400 Amalekites who escaped represent the residual evil that remains unfinished — to be dealt with at the end by the Messiah Himself. The picture today of “devoured straw” is, in this reading, the dealing with that 400.

六、列國彼此毀滅 ── Yoma 第十章VI. The Nations Destroy Each Other — Yoma Chapter 10

塔木德 Yoma 第十章（即 Yoma 10a，與本系列第一篇文章中我們提到的同一段）說：「波斯注定要落入羅馬手中。」拉比沙皮拉把這節讀為今日地緣政治的預言：波斯（伊朗）與羅馬（西方／基督教世界）將彼此牽動，最終彼此毀滅；只有雅各與約瑟仍然站立。 Talmud Yoma Chapter 10 (specifically Yoma 10a, the same passage we visited in the first article in this series) says: “Persia is destined to fall into the hands of Rome.” Rabbi Shapira reads this for today's geopolitics: Persia (Iran) and Rome (the West / the Christian nations) will pull on each other, and ultimately destroy each other; only Jacob and Joseph remain standing.

這幅圖景與俄巴底亞書 1:18 的圖景合一：以掃家如稭被燒盡——而「燒」的火焰，是約瑟（Yeshua）。 This picture coincides with the picture of Obadiah 1:18: the house of Esau burned as stubble — and the flame that does the burning is Joseph (Yeshua).

七、對基督徒的意義VII. What This Means for Christians

第一，當以色列的敵人自己引用聖經預言的字句作為自己行動的代號時，我們應該停下來思考：他們無意識地宣告了什麼？真主黨以為自己在說「我們要燒盡以色列」，但他們所引用的句子在原版（俄巴底亞書）中說的是反過來：燒盡的是以掃，火焰是約瑟。 First, when Israel's enemies quote the very text of biblical prophecy as the codename for their own operations, we should pause: what have they unwittingly announced? Hezbollah meant the line as “we shall devour Israel.” But the original text in Obadiah says the opposite: the one devoured is Esau; the flame is Joseph.

第二，「約瑟 = 約書亞 = 耶書亞」這個米大示連結，給基督徒對耶書亞的信仰一個極為深刻的猶太根基。我們所信的這位不是一位被新約「發明」的彌賽亞——祂是米大示中早已指出的那位。 Second, the midrashic chain “Joseph = Yehoshua = Yeshua” gives Christian faith in Yeshua a profoundly Jewish foundation. The Yeshua we believe in is not a Messiah “invented” by the New Testament. He is the One the midrash had already named.

第三，俄巴底亞書 1:18 給今日基督徒一個極具盼望的視角：在一個感覺上「世界在崩裂」的時刻，先知早已說：不是雅各被燒盡，是以掃被燒盡；不是約瑟被吞噬，是稭被吞噬。無論新聞如何令人焦慮，神的話已先一步給了結局。 Third, Obadiah 1:18 offers Christians today a profoundly hopeful perspective. In an hour when the world feels like it is splitting apart, the prophet already said: it is not Jacob who is burned up — it is Esau; it is not Joseph who is devoured — it is the stubble. However anxious the news, the word of God has spoken the ending in advance.

八、關於分辨VIII. A Word on Discernment

把「以掃 = 阿拉伯—伊斯蘭聯盟」的完全等同，是一個拉比傳統中常見的類型學應用。聖經文本中以掃是一個具體的古代民族（以東）。把這個身分擴展到當代政治勢力，是應用而不是字面解經。同樣，「約瑟 = 約書亞 = 耶書亞」的米大示連結是一個極為有力的神學共鳴，但它建立在希伯來名字學上，不是俄巴底亞書本身的字面陳述。 Equating “Esau” with the Arab-Islamic alliance is a classical rabbinic typological application. In the biblical text, Esau is a specific ancient people (Edom). Extending the identity to a contemporary political bloc is application, not plain exegesis. Likewise, the “Joseph = Yehoshua = Yeshua” midrashic chain is a powerful theological resonance, but it depends on Hebrew onomastics, not on Obadiah's plain text.

話雖如此，真主黨自己選擇了這個名字這個歷史事實是不可否認的——它確實在我們眼前發生了。如何解讀這個事實，每位讀者可以自己權衡，但事實本身值得停下來想一想。 That said, the historical fact that Hezbollah itself chose this name is undeniable — it really happened in front of us. How to read that fact is each reader's own weighing — but the fact itself is worth pausing over.

九、結語IX. Closing

「以掃家必為碎稭。火焰是約瑟。約瑟是約書亞。約書亞是耶書亞。願祂速速顯現。」 “The house of Esau shall be stubble. The flame is Joseph. Joseph is Yehoshua. Yehoshua is Yeshua. May He be revealed soon.”
