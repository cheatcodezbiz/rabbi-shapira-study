F-35「Adir」奧秘 ── 從以賽亞書 10:34 到 11:1 的彌賽亞之箭 The F-35 “Adir” Mystery — From Isaiah 10:34 to 11:1, the Messiah's Arrow

以色列空軍 F-35 戰機的希伯來代號是「Adir」(אַדִּיר，「大能者」)。當這架戰機在德黑蘭擊落伊朗戰機的那一刻，它正在字面上應驗以賽亞書 10:34——「黎巴嫩的森林必被大能者 (Adir) 砍倒」——而下一節立刻接著：「從耶西的本必發一條，從他根生的枝子必結果實」（彌賽亞）。 The Israeli Air Force's F-35 carries the Hebrew callsign “Adir” (אַדִּיר, “the Mighty One”). The moment that jet downed an Iranian aircraft over Tehran, it was literally fulfilling Isaiah 10:34 — “the forest of Lebanon shall fall by the Mighty One (Adir)” — and the very next verse continues: “There shall come forth a shoot from the stump of Jesse… and a Branch shall grow from his roots” (the Messiah).

沙皮拉拉比博士 · Ahavat Ammi 事工 Rabbi Dr. Itzhak Shapira · Ahavat Ammi Ministries

2024 年八月，以色列空軍的一架 F-35 戰機在德黑蘭上空擊落了一架伊朗 Yak-130。這在 F-35 的歷史上是首次實戰擊落他國戰機的紀錄。在新聞媒體上，這只是又一場以伊衝突的篇章。在拉比沙皮拉的眼中——這架戰機在以色列空軍中的希伯來代號是 「Adir」(אַדִּיר，「大能者」)——它的行動字面上應驗了以賽亞書 10:34：「黎巴嫩的森林必被『Adir』(大能者) 砍倒。」而下一節（11:1）立刻說：「從耶西的本必發一條，從他根生的枝子必結果實。」「Adir」之後，緊接著的是「枝子」(Netzer，נצר) ──── 即彌賽亞耶書亞自己。 In August 2024, an Israeli Air Force F-35 downed an Iranian Yak-130 over Tehran. It was the first time in the F-35's history that the platform had recorded a real-world air-to-air kill. In the news, it was another chapter of the Iran-Israel war. To Rabbi Shapira's eye — the IAF's Hebrew callsign for the F-35 is “Adir” (אַדִּיר, “the Mighty One”) — the action literally fulfilled Isaiah 10:34: “the forest of Lebanon shall fall by the Mighty One (Adir).” And the very next verse (11:1) continues: “There shall come forth a shoot from the stump of Jesse… and a Branch shall grow from his roots.” After “Adir” comes “the Branch” (Netzer, נצר) — the Messiah Yeshua Himself.

一、F-35 的希伯來代號 ── 「Adir」I. The F-35's Hebrew Callsign — “Adir”

F-35 在以色列空軍中的正式代號是「Adir」(אַדִּיר)。這個代號並非隨意選擇——以色列國防部在 2014 年正式宣布這個名字時，特別說明這是引自《詩篇》與《以賽亞書》中描述「神的大能」的那個字。「Adir」直譯是「大能的、威嚴的、令人敬畏的」；在聖經中常用來形容神自己（詩篇 8:1：「耶和華我們的主啊，祢的名在全地何其美！(Adir)」）。 The F-35 in Israeli Air Force service carries the official callsign “Adir” (אַדִּיר). This was not a casual choice — when the Israeli Ministry of Defense announced the name in 2014, they specifically noted it was drawn from Psalms and Isaiah, where the word describes the might of God Himself. “Adir” means “mighty, majestic, awe-inspiring” — in Scripture it is most often a divine attribute (Psalm 8:1: “O LORD, our Lord, how majestic (Adir) is Your name in all the earth!”).

把神的名字賦予一架戰機是一個極具份量的選擇——它不只是公關修辭，而是以色列軍隊明白：這架戰機所執行的任務，本質上是神聖戰爭的一部分。當這架「Adir」在 2024 年八月擊落伊朗戰機時，它就以這個名字進入了歷史。 Giving a fighter jet a divine name is a weighty decision — not PR rhetoric, but an acknowledgement by the Israeli military that the missions this aircraft will fly are part of holy war. When this “Adir” downed the Iranian jet in August 2024, it entered history under that name.

二、以賽亞書 10:34 ── 「Adir 砍倒黎巴嫩」II. Isaiah 10:34 — “The Mighty One Will Fell Lebanon”

「稠密的樹林祂要用鐵器砍下；黎巴嫩的樹木必被大能者 (Adir) 所砍倒。」——以賽亞書 10:34 “He will cut down the thickets of the forest with iron, and Lebanon shall fall by the Mighty One (Adir).”— Isaiah 10:34

在以賽亞書的上下文中，「黎巴嫩」是亞述帝國的象徵——一個高大、驕傲、不可一世的森林帝國，神將親自把它砍倒。「黎巴嫩的森林」在拉比傳統中常被讀為以色列的敵人——任何時代崛起、自以為不可戰勝的勢力。「Adir」就是那位執行砍伐工作的大能者。 In Isaiah's context, “Lebanon” is the symbol of the Assyrian empire — a tall, proud, seemingly unstoppable forest of an empire that God Himself will cut down. “The forest of Lebanon” is read in rabbinic tradition as Israel's enemies in any age — every power that rises and imagines itself invincible. “Adir” is the One who carries out the cutting.

今天的對應極為直接：F-35「Adir」確實在黎巴嫩上空執行任務（多次擊毀真主黨指揮設施）並在德黑蘭上空擊落伊朗戰機。希伯來文的字根、聖經的預言、與當代的軍事行動，在同一個字之下被縫合在一起。 The contemporary correspondence is striking: the F-35 “Adir” actually flies over Lebanon (repeatedly striking Hezbollah command-and-control) and downed the Iranian jet over Tehran. The Hebrew root, the prophet's prediction, and the contemporary military action are stitched together under one word.

三、以賽亞書 11:1 ── 「Adir」之後是「Netzer」III. Isaiah 11:1 — After “Adir” Comes “Netzer”

「從耶西的本必發一條 (Choter)，從他根生的枝子 (Netzer) 必結果實。耶和華的靈必住在他身上⋯⋯」——以賽亞書 11:1-2 “There shall come forth a shoot (Choter) from the stump of Jesse, and a Branch (Netzer) shall grow out of his roots. And the Spirit of the LORD shall rest upon Him…”— Isaiah 11:1-2

拉比沙皮拉指出一個關鍵的事實：以賽亞書 10:34 與 11:1 在希伯來聖經中是緊接的兩節。先知並不是在 10:34「砍倒森林」的圖景之後另起一段——他立即接著說「從耶西的本必發一條」。換言之：「Adir 砍倒之後」與「彌賽亞興起」是同一個事件的兩個面向。 Rabbi Shapira presses a critical point: Isaiah 10:34 and 11:1 are back-to-back verses in the Hebrew text. The prophet does not start a new section after the “felling of the forest” in 10:34 — he immediately follows with “a shoot from the stump of Jesse.” In other words: “the Mighty One's cutting” and “the Messiah's rising” are two faces of the same event.

「枝子」這個字的希伯來文是 Netzer（נצר）——這是「拿撒勒」(Natzeret，נצרת) 的字根，也是新約稱耶書亞為「拿撒勒人」(Notzri) 的字根。馬太福音 2:23 明說：「後來到了一座城，名叫拿撒勒，就住在那裡，這是要應驗先知所說，他將稱為拿撒勒人的話了。」這節「先知所說」一直令解經家困惑——舊約找不到「拿撒勒人」這個詞。但若按拉比傳統，馬太指的就是以賽亞書 11:1 中的「Netzer」。 The Hebrew for “branch” here is Netzer (נצר) — the same root as Nazareth (Natzeret), and the same root behind the New Testament's title for Yeshua: “the Nazarene” (Notzri). Matthew 2:23 says: “and he came and dwelt in a city called Nazareth, that it might be fulfilled which was spoken by the prophets, ‘He shall be called a Nazarene.’” This “spoken by the prophets” has long puzzled commentators — there is no “Nazarene” in the Old Testament. By the rabbinic reading, Matthew is pointing precisely at the Netzer of Isaiah 11:1.

四、Klipot 的擊破 ── 為彌賽亞鋪路IV. Shattering the Klipot — Clearing the Way for Messiah

在卡巴拉傳統中，Klipot（קליפות，「殼」）是邪惡力量的外衣。當神「砍倒森林」時，祂在做的不只是政治、軍事意義上的勝利——祂在擊破邪惡的外殼，讓彌賽亞之光得以透出。 In Kabbalistic tradition, Klipot (קליפות, “shells”) are the husks that wrap evil. When God “fells the forest,” He is not only winning a political-military victory — He is shattering the husks of evil so that the light of the Messiah can break through.

「Adir」這個字的另一個層次——Adir'ut（אדירות，「大能之至」、「至聖之聖」）——指向神內裡的光，這光擊破 Klipot，並引出彌賽亞的光。當以色列空軍的 Adir 在天空中行動時，地上的 Klipot 一層層被剝開——不是因為以色列的飛行員多麼英勇，而是因為神正在擊破。 Another layer of “Adir” — Adir'ut (אדירות, “the supreme might,” “holiness of holinesses”) — points to the inner light of God, the light that breaks through the Klipot and ushers in the Messiah's light. When the IAF's Adir moves in the sky, the Klipot on the ground are being peeled away, layer by layer — not because Israeli pilots are uniquely brave, but because God is breaking through.

五、Mor-Dror 與 Mordechai ── 出埃及記 30:23 的密碼V. Mor-Dror and Mordechai — The Code in Exodus 30:23

拉比沙皮拉接著從本週妥拉段落（《Bo 段落》）中拉出另一條密碼線。出埃及記 30:23 列舉了製造聖膏油的香料：「上品的沒藥 (Mor-Dror，מור־דרור) 五百舍客勒、香肉桂二百五十舍客勒、菖蒲二百五十舍客勒。」 Rabbi Shapira draws another thread from this week's Torah portion (Parashat Bo). Exodus 30:23 lists the ingredients of the holy anointing oil: “pure myrrh (Mor-Dror, מור־דרור) 500 shekels, sweet cinnamon 250 shekels, calamus 250 shekels.”

Mor-Dror（「自由的沒藥」）這個詞在阿拉米文中對應 Modia——而 Modia 與「Mordechai」(מרדכי) 在字根上相連。也就是說：以斯帖記中那位拯救以色列脫離哈曼之手的Mordechai，他名字的字根就藏在聖膏油中——而聖膏油是用來膏抹君王、祭司、彌賽亞的。 Mor-Dror (“liberating myrrh”) corresponds in Aramaic to Modia — and Modia shares its root with Mordechai (מרדכי). In other words: the Mordechai who delivered Israel from Haman in the book of Esther carries, in his very name, the root of the holy anointing oil — the oil used to anoint kings, priests, and the Messiah.

拉比沙皮拉的應用：當亞瑪力（哈曼的後裔，今天的伊朗）在 F-35「Adir」之下倒下時，「Mordechai」(膏抹之人) 就要出現——也就是那位被膏的彌賽亞。聖膏油的字根、以斯帖的故事、與末世圖景，在這個希伯來字根的層次上連成一線。 Rabbi Shapira's application: when Amalek (the descendant of Haman; today's Iran) falls under the F-35 “Adir,” “Mordechai” — the anointed one — appears. That is, the Messiah, the Anointed One. The holy oil's root, Esther's story, and the end-time picture line up at this Hebrew-root level.

六、對基督徒的意義VI. What This Means for Christians

第一，以賽亞書 10:34 → 11:1 的緊接性是一個常被基督徒忽略的細節。我們讀以賽亞書 11 章「從耶西的本必發一條」時，往往把它從上下文中抽離；但希伯來聖經本身把它放在「Adir 砍倒森林」之後——因為彌賽亞之來臨，是在邪惡被擊破的後一刻。 First, the back-to-back nature of Isaiah 10:34 → 11:1 is a detail Christians often miss. We tend to read Isaiah 11 (“a shoot from the stump of Jesse”) as if it stood alone. The Hebrew text places it immediately after “Adir fells the forest” — because the Messiah's coming follows, the very next moment, after evil is shattered.

第二，「Netzer = 拿撒勒」這個連結，給馬太福音 2:23 一個極為清楚的解經。它告訴我們：耶書亞所稱「拿撒勒人」的稱號，本身就是以賽亞書 11:1 中那位「枝子」（彌賽亞）的應驗。「拿撒勒」這個小村莊的名字承載著彌賽亞論的整個重量。 Second, the “Netzer = Nazareth” link gives Matthew 2:23 a remarkably clear reading. It tells us: the title “Nazarene” that names Yeshua is itself the fulfilment of the “Branch” (Messiah) of Isaiah 11:1. The little village of Nazareth carries the whole weight of messianic prophecy in its name.

第三，「Mor-Dror → Mordechai → 受膏者」這條字根鏈，告訴我們：聖膏油的奧秘從出埃及一直貫穿到末世。基督 (Christos / Mashiach) 字面意思就是「受膏者」。當以色列在地上經歷「Adir」與「Mordechai」的圖景時，基督徒應當看見：同一位基督正在地上的兩個層面同時行動。 Third, the chain “Mor-Dror → Mordechai → the Anointed One” tells us that the mystery of the holy oil runs from Exodus all the way to the end. Christ (Christos / Mashiach) literally means “the anointed one.” As Israel experiences the “Adir” and the “Mordechai” motifs on the earthly stage, Christians should see: the same Anointed One is acting on both layers of the same earth at once.

七、關於分辨VII. A Word on Discernment

F-35 的代號叫「Adir」是歷史事實；以色列國防部 2014 年正式宣布的；可以在維基百科上查到。這不是 Drash。但「以賽亞書 10:34 → F-35 擊落伊朗戰機」的對應，是一種應用層面的解經——它建立在希伯來字根上的共鳴極其有力，但仍不是「字面預言」。 That the F-35's IAF callsign is “Adir” is historical fact — formally announced by the Israeli MoD in 2014; you can verify it on Wikipedia. That part is not drash. But the mapping of “Isaiah 10:34 → F-35 downing the Iranian jet” is an applied reading — its Hebrew-root resonance is real and powerful, but it is not “literal prediction.”

話雖如此，「邪惡被擊破之後緊接著彌賽亞興起」這個更大的圖景——就是以賽亞書 10–11 章本身的圖景——是聖經自己的字面教導，並非穿鑿。把這個圖景應用到我們今天所看見的事件上，是一個合理的盼望，不是強解。 That said, the larger picture — after evil is shattered, the Messiah immediately rises — is the picture Isaiah 10–11 itself plainly paints; it is not strained. Applying that picture to events we are watching today is a reasonable hope, not a forced reading.

八、結語 ── Adir 之後是 NetzerVIII. Closing — After Adir Comes Netzer

「Adir 已經在天上行動了。下一個出現的，是 Netzer ── 從耶西的本所發出的枝子。願祂速速顯現。Am Yisrael Chai。」 “Adir is already moving in the heavens. What comes next is Netzer — the Branch from the stump of Jesse. May He be revealed soon. Am Yisrael Chai.”
