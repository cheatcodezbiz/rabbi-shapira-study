沙皮拉拉比博士Rabbi Dr. Itzhak Shapira

以撒·沙皮拉拉比博士 Rabbi Dr. Itzhak Shapira
爱我民事工 · 耶示瓦·书武 创办人 Founder · Ahavat Ammi & Yeshivat Shuvu
爱我民事工 (Ahavat Ammi Ministries) · 「为爱我的子民」 Ahavat Ammi Ministries · "For the Love of My People"

在妥拉的字里行间，他找到了弥赛亚 Within the Pages of Torah, He Found the Messiah

以撒（Tzahi）·沙皮拉在以色列一个传统的赛法迪（Sephardic）犹太家庭中出生成长。从年少起，他便沉浸于拉比文献与希伯来文本的研究之中，在那些古老文本的深处，他发现了一个令他震惊的真理——犹太人所盼望的弥赛亚，在圣经与拉比传统中留下了清晰的印记。 Tzahi (Itzhak) Shapira was born and raised in Israel in a traditional Sephardic Jewish home. From a young age he immersed himself in Rabbinic literature and Hebrew texts, and within those ancient writings he discovered a truth that astonished him — that the Messiah Israel awaited had left unmistakable marks throughout Scripture and Rabbinic tradition.

他是爱我民事工（Ahavat Ammi Ministries）及耶示瓦·书武（Yeshivat Shuvu）的创办人，致力于以正统的犹太视角呈现拿撒勒的耶书亚（Yeshua）作为犹太弥赛亚的圣经根据。其著作《洁净猪的归来》引用了横跨2,000年的拉比文献，许多首次被翻译成英文，被视为此领域的重要学术文献。 He is the founder of Ahavat Ammi Ministries and Yeshivat Shuvu, dedicated to presenting the biblical case for Yeshua of Nazareth as the Jewish Messiah from an authentic Jewish perspective. His landmark work, The Return of the Kosher Pig, draws on 2,000 years of Rabbinic literature — much translated into English for the first time.
